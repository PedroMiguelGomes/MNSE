Details

In this tutorial we are going to focus mainly on the Principles of Visual Processing in Matlab.

The objective is to introduce you different representations of digital visual signals and to some basic visual processing techniques using Matlab.

Matlab scripts can be seen on the side of the video and the images we are using can be downloaded bellow. The output will consist of processed versions of those images, which we’re going to analyse with you to interpret the effects of the conducted processing operations.


Note: The majority of the images has the bitmap format (.bmp) which means that each pixel is represented by three RGB eight bit values, so in total 24 bits per pixel.
